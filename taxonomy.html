Scope, Taxonomy & Evidence

Open/closed and black-box/white-box are orthogonal dimensions. This page defines access levels, dependency types, evidence grades, and the boundary between verified distillation, general model derivation, and allegation.

Access is not openness

TargetOutputsLogprobsRepresentationsWeights/checkpoints
Commercial closed modelUsually availableSometimes top-k onlyRareUnavailable externally
Open-weight modelAvailableFull logits locallyHidden states, attention, routingFull parameters; sometimes multiple checkpoints
Use “open-weight” precisely: available weights do not necessarily imply open training data, full code, or unrestricted licensing.
